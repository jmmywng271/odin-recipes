Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or the typical lasgna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients.

Typically lasagna is made with meat, sauce, and cheese. Be careful as lasgna can be really calories dense.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Spices and seasonings

Steps

  1. Prep your ingredients
  2. Layer your lasagna by alternating between noodle, meat, sauce, and cheese
  3. Place lasagna into a pre-heated oven at 375 degrees and cook for 50 min
