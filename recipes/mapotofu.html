Mapo Tofu

Description

A traditional Sichuan dish, mapo tofu is made with simmered medium-firm silken tofu flavored with fermented bean paste, beef, plenty of red-hot roasred chili oil, and a handful of Sichuan peppercorns.

WARNING! If you do not like spicy, this dish is not for you.

Ingredients

  • Tofu... duh
  • Peppercorns! And other spices
  • Beef
  • Spicy bean paste
  • Corn starch
  • Chicken stock

Steps

  1. Cook your peppercorns in oil for 1-2 minutes
  2. Add your ground beef and cook until brown
  3. Add the spicy bean paste and your other spices
  4. Add your brother and let it simmer for a bit
  5. Add your tofu, voila you are done!
